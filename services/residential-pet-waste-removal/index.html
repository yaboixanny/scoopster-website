Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule service?

Most homeowners choose weekly service to keep waste from building up. Bi-weekly works if you have a larger yard or fewer dogs. One-time cleanups are great for seasonal catch-up or move-in situations.

Do I need to be home?

Nope. As long as we can access your yard through an unlocked gate or side entrance, we'll handle everything while you're away. We send text notifications when we're on our way and email confirmations when done.

What do you do with the waste?

We bag it and haul it away for proper disposal. Nothing is left in your trash cans or on your property.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. You can cancel or pause service anytime with no fees or penalties. We earn your business every week through reliable service.

What if my dog is aggressive or nervous?

Just keep them inside during our visit. We send a text notification before arrival so you have time to secure your pet. Safety for both your dog and our team is the priority.
