Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service all parts of the Mountain?

Yes, we cover all Mountain neighbourhoods from the escarpment to Rymal and beyond. Our extensive service area includes Upper Gage, Concession, Limeridge, Mountain Brow, and all areas in between. No matter where you live on the Mountain, we've got you covered.

What day would my pickup be?

We assign service days by area for maximum route efficiency, which helps us keep prices low and service reliable. Once you sign up, you'll get a consistent weekly day that we stick to every week. You'll always know exactly when to expect us.

Can you access my backyard if my gate is locked?

Absolutely. We can work with combination locks, hidden keys, or any access arrangement that works for you. Just let us know your preferred method during signup, and we'll make sure our team has the information they need to access your yard safely and securely.
