Frequently Asked Questions

Do larger Ancaster lots cost more?

Sometimes, yes. We provide custom quotes based on your yard size and the number of dogs you have. While our starting rates are very competitive, larger Ancaster properties with more extensive cleanup needs may be quoted slightly higher to ensure we provide thorough service. We'll always give you a transparent quote upfront before you commit.

Do you service the rural edges of Ancaster?

Most of Ancaster falls within our regular service area, including many rural and semi-rural properties. If you're located on the outskirts or in a more remote area, reach out to us and we'll confirm whether we can include you in our routes. We're always looking to expand our coverage.

Can I book service for just spring and summer?

Absolutely! Seasonal service is one of our most popular options. Many customers prefer service during warmer months when they use their yards more frequently. You can pause your service in the fall and restart it in the spring with no hassle or fees.
